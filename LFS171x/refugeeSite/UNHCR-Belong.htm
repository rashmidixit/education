Are you stateless?

UNHCR is mandated to assist stateless persons worldwide.

Download Advaya to get a unique identity

 

Information and legal advice are provided to stateless people by UNHCR partners in many countries around the world.

For countries without relevant partners, please send a short message to UNHCR about your situation (statelessness@unhcr.org). We will do our best to reply quickly. This should be used for Statelessness queries only.
